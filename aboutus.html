About us

As Film Geek's hosts, we are three passionate movie fans who are excited to share our enthusiasm for TV shows and films to everyone. This podcast, where we discuss the best TV shows and films recommendations, came up from our constant talks about the newest films and our favorite series. Although our backgrounds may be different Our shared passion of storytelling connects us, even if one of us is a huge fan of anime and the other is addicted to blockbusters and binge worthy tv shows.

 

We provide plenty of opinions, from in depth research to lighthearted, funny conversations. We are aware of the overwhelming nature of selecting the ideal movie or TV show, especially when our emotion is a major factor. This podcast was created with the intention of supporting you on your path by offering sincere, targeted guidance based on what you are feeling at the moment. We'll make sure you find something that exactly fits your mood, whether you want to check out a new release or revisit a classic.

When it comes to talking about movies and TV shows on FG, we also want to make viewers feel comfortable. One emotion, one movie, or one episode at a time, let's all celebrate the cinematic magic together!
